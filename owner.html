Security & password
  • Change your credentials: open dev-tools console and run await Security.hashFor('your-email@example.com', 'your-new-password'), then paste the two printed hashes into OWNER_EMAIL_HASH and OWNER_HASH in assets/js/security.js and redeploy.
  • Your session expires automatically after hours of inactivity; use Log out on shared devices.
  • This client-side gate is best-effort. For server-enforced security, follow the Firebase upgrade in the README.
